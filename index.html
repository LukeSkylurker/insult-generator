The Highbrow Insult Generator is meant to give people the opportunity to express their feelings without stooping to the level of their inferior counterparts. Press the "Generate!" button until you feel you find the insult that reflects the true severity of your displeasure.

If you should have any questions, or would like to see a word or phrase added, please feel free leave a comment in the section above.

The Highbrow Insult Generator will continue to undergo many iterations. Bookmark this page and check back often to see new functionality, words, and (of course) insults.
